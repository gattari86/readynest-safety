Section 5

Website Messaging

The content you put together is already in a great place. Your instinct to lead with the childcare angle is exactly right. Here is why:

We looked at every CPR training provider serving the Katy and West Houston market. What we found:

  • Most competitors are general CPR companies that list childcare as one of many audiences
  • Nobody bundles CPR + continuing education + emergency preparedness in one place
  • Nobody positions from inside the childcare world -- they all market to childcare from the outside
  • Online-only CPR does not meet Texas childcare licensing requirements -- your hands-on, in-person training is exactly what the state requires

Your strongest angle on the website:

"Safety training built from inside the childcare world -- by a paramedic and a preschool director."

This is a true, defensible position that no competitor can replicate. The combination of Sharief's emergency medical experience and Stephanie's hands-on preschool leadership is genuinely unique in this market. We will use your content as the foundation and sharpen the language to make this angle come through clearly.
