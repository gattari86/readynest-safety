How to Add Your Signature in Gmail

  1. Click inside the dashed box above your name and press Ctrl+A (Windows) or Cmd+A (Mac) to select just the signature content inside the box. Or manually click and drag to select only your signature.
  2. Copy it with Ctrl+C / Cmd+C
  3. Open Gmail and go to Settings (gear icon) > See all settings
  4. Scroll down to the Signature section
  5. Click + Create new and name it (e.g., "ReadyNest")
  6. Click inside the signature editor box and paste with Ctrl+V / Cmd+V
  7. Scroll down and click Save Changes

Tip: Only select the content inside the dashed box -- not the label above it. The logo, links, and formatting will all carry over when you paste into Gmail.
