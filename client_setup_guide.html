Account Setup Guide

ReadyNest Safety Training  |  Prepared by Poppy Marketing

Before we launch your website, there are a few free accounts that need to be set up. This guide walks you through each one step by step. Everything here is free -- no credit card needed.

What You Need and When

Account What It Does When
Formspree Powers the "Request Training" contact form on your website. Submissions go straight to your email. Set up now
Google Analytics Tracks who visits your website, where they come from, and what they do. Helps you understand what is working. Set up now
Google Search Console Shows how your site appears in Google search results. Helps us monitor and improve your visibility over time. At launch

Website hosting is handled on our end -- no account needed from you for that. Your GoDaddy domain settings will need a small update at launch time, and we will walk you through that separately.

1

Formspree

What this is: Formspree handles the contact form on your website. When someone fills out the "Request Training" form, the submission goes directly to your email inbox. You own the account and the data.

  1. Go to https://formspree.io and click "Get Started" (top right).
  2. Click "Sign up with Google" and sign in with your Google Workspace account (info@readynestsafety.com). This is the easiest option since you already have Google Workspace.
  3. Once you are logged in, you will land on your Dashboard. Click the "+" button or "New Form".
  4. Give the form a name: Training Request
  5. For the recipient email, make sure it is set to info@readynestsafety.com (this is where form submissions will be delivered).
  6. Click "Create Form".
  7. After the form is created, you will see a detail page. Look for the Integration section. You will see a Form Endpoint URL that looks like this: https://formspree.io/f/xyznabcd
  8. Copy that endpoint URL and send it to Ricardo.
Send back to Ricardo:
  • The Form Endpoint URL (starts with https://formspree.io/f/)
Good to know: The free plan includes 50 form submissions per month, which is more than enough to start. If volume grows, upgrades start at $10/month.
2

Google Analytics

What this is: Google Analytics tracks how people find and use your website -- how many visitors you get, where they come from, which pages they view, and whether they submit the contact form. You own the account and the data. We will manage it for you.

  1. Go to https://analytics.google.com and sign in with your Google Workspace account (info@readynestsafety.com).
  2. If this is your first time, Google will ask you to accept the Terms of Service. Read through and accept.
  3. Click "Start Measuring".
  4. Account Name: Enter ReadyNest Safety Training. Leave the data sharing checkboxes at their defaults. Click "Next".
  5. Property Name: Enter ReadyNest Website. Set the time zone to Central Time (US) and currency to US Dollar (USD). Click "Next".
  6. Business Details: For industry, select something close like "Business & Industrial". For size, select "Small". Click "Next".
  7. Business Objectives: Check "Generate leads" and "Get baseline reports". Click "Create".
  8. Accept the GA4 Terms of Service if prompted again.
  9. Set up a Data Stream: Select "Web". Enter your website URL: readynestsafety.com. Enter a stream name: ReadyNest Web. Leave "Enhanced Measurement" toggled ON. Click "Create Stream".
  10. You will now see a Measurement ID at the top of the stream details. It starts with G- followed by letters and numbers (example: G-ABC123XYZ). Copy this ID and send it to Ricardo.

Now add Ricardo as an Editor so he can manage analytics for you:

  1. Click the gear icon (bottom left corner) to go to Admin.
  2. Under the Property column (middle), click "Property Access Management".
  3. Click the blue "+" button (top right) and select "Add Users".
  4. Enter the email: ricardo@poppymarketingandconsulting.com
  5. Under Standard Roles, select "Editor".
  6. Click "Add" (top right).
Send back to Ricardo:
  • The Measurement ID (starts with G-)
  • Confirmation that ricardo@poppymarketingandconsulting.com was added as Editor
Important: Make sure you are signed into your business Google account (info@readynestsafety.com), not a personal Gmail. This ensures your business owns the analytics data long-term.
3

Google Search Console

What this is: Google Search Console shows you how your website appears in Google search results -- which searches bring people to your site, how often you show up, and whether Google can find all your pages. You own the account and the data. We will manage it for you.

Timing: This one happens at launch. We will let you know when it is time.

  1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console and sign in with your Google Workspace account (info@readynestsafety.com).
  2. Click "Add Property" (dropdown at the top left, or you will be prompted on first visit).
  3. On the left side, select "Domain". Enter: readynestsafety.com (no https, no www). Click "Continue".
  4. Google will show you a TXT record to verify you own the domain. It will look something like: google-site-verification=XXXXXXXXXXXXXX. Copy the entire string.
  5. Now go to GoDaddy to add this record:
    - Log in to https://www.godaddy.com
    - Click your profile icon (top right) and go to "My Products"
    - Find readynestsafety.com and click "DNS" (or "Manage DNS")
    - Click "Add New Record"
    - Type: TXT
    - Name: @
    - Value: paste the full google-site-verification string
    - TTL: leave as default
    - Click "Save"
  6. Go back to Google Search Console and click "Verify". If it does not verify right away, wait 15-30 minutes and try again -- DNS changes take a little time to spread.

Once verified, add Ricardo as a user:

  1. In Search Console, click "Settings" (gear icon, bottom left).
  2. Click "Users and permissions".
  3. Click "Add User".
  4. Enter: ricardo@poppymarketingandconsulting.com
  5. Set permission to "Full".
  6. Click "Add".
Send back to Ricardo:
  • Confirmation that the domain is verified in Search Console
  • Confirmation that ricardo@poppymarketingandconsulting.com was added as a Full user
Do not worry if verification takes time. DNS changes usually take 15-30 minutes but can occasionally take up to a few hours. If you get an error, wait and try the "Verify" button again later.
