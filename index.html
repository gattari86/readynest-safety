Our Story

About ReadyNest

ReadyNest Safety was founded by a husband-and-wife team committed to improving safety in childcare environments.

Sharief

Sharief is a licensed paramedic and Field Training Officer with years of experience in emergency medical response and provider development. He holds a Bachelor's degree in EMS Administration and has trained emergency responders and community members in lifesaving skills throughout his career. Through his work in emergency medical services, he has seen firsthand why preparedness and early intervention matter.

Stephanie

Stephanie brings extensive experience as a preschool director, providing valuable insight into the operational needs, regulatory requirements, and day-to-day safety challenges faced by childcare centers.

Together, they created ReadyNest to bridge the gap between emergency medical expertise and childcare leadership. The result: practical safety training and preparedness programs designed specifically for the people who care for children every day.
